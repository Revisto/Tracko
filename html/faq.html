What is Tracko?

Tracko is a system which helps people to track the series or other things that is being watched or listened. What it does is to tell you how much of which episode of which season you have watched to avoid rewatching or relistening episodes or podcasts.

Is There any Api to use?

Not yet but as soon as possible we will launch it and make this system possible for developers and programmers all around the world.

How to use Tracko?

Easy-peasy just sign up or sign in and then add your series or podcasts or whatever and update them from the upside bar.

Is Tracko OpenSource?

It is FOSS and everyone cann use it free or edit it or even make their own version of TRACKO and launch it on therir own sites.
